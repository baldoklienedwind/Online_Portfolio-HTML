Portfolio

Developed SwapMate, a full-featured e-commerce website tailored for 2nd hand items, specifically targeting students seeking affordable and discounted products. The platform includes a secure user authentication system, allowing students to create and manage their accounts safely. It features comprehensive product listings with detailed descriptions, images, and pricing information, making it easy for users to browse and find items of interest. The site also includes a fully functional shopping cart that supports adding, updating, and removing products, as well as a streamlined checkout process. The design is user-friendly and responsive, ensuring an optimal browsing experience across all devices.

The site features a dynamic content management system, enabling users to easily create, edit, and manage their posts, including images and captions. The layout is inspired by modern social media designs, ensuring an intuitive and seamless user experience. The platform is fully responsive, offering consistent functionality and aesthetics across all devices, from desktops to mobile phones. Additionally, the website includes social interaction features such as liking, sharing, and saving posts, fostering an interactive and community-driven environment.
